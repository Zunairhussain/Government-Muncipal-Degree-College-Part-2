Principal's Message

Muhammad Iqbal Chohan 2022-12-31

Principal Message

After serving in Education department as a teacher for a prolonged period of 32 years, I have nourished certain dreams and ideas in my mind for the training and education of our new generation and to enhance the standard of Government educational Institutions Now when I am at the twilight of my career, Allah Almighty has blessed me with this opportunity in the shape of my tenure as Principal to materialize my dreams. I feel the love of this college in my bones as I have spent my childhood as a student in this prestigious Institution. Right from the first day of my tenure I left no stone unturned in uplifting the standard of this College paying full attention and concentration to academics, discipline, conduct of classes, beautification of college, curricular and cocurricular activities. Now my devotion, hard work, labor and toil is being reflected in all the realms of college life as mentioned above. I advise my dear students to remain closest to your books in all the circumstances as sword of knowledge is stronger to the metallic sword and join your hands with me for lifting this College to the peaks of perfection. May Allah bless all of us with health and success to accomplish this noble cause. to the peaks of perfection.
